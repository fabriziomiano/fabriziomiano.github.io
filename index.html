About

I'm a Business Analytics Manager at STMicroelectronics, where I design data-driven solutions that help sales and operations teams plan, decide, and perform more effectively.

Before focusing on business analytics, I worked across data governance, solution design, and consulting — from Coca-Cola HBC to Capgemini and the public sector. My background in experimental physics and research at CERN taught me how to think analytically, solve complex problems, and build scalable systems that work in the real world. I'm passionate about making data meaningful — not just accessible.
