About

A data leader shaped by research, delivery, and real business constraints.

I started from physics, where evidence matters and complexity is normal. Years in the ATLAS/CERN environment taught me to reason carefully, communicate uncertainty, and build analysis that can survive scrutiny.

Over time I moved closer to business: consulting, data governance, BI platforms, and now semiconductor sales and marketing analytics. I still like the technical depth, but I care most about whether a decision becomes clearer, faster, and more defensible.
